Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026
🔒 Your data stays on your device. We never share, sell or access your client data.

1. Data We Collect

CAdesk.ai is designed with privacy first. Client data you enter (names, PAN, GSTIN, etc.) is stored locally on your device using browser storage. We do not transmit this data to our servers.

We collect minimal usage data: number of documents generated (anonymized), browser type, and general location (country level) for improving our service.

2. How We Use Data

Document drafts are processed through our AI backend (Cloudflare Workers) and are not stored after processing. The text you input for document generation is used solely to generate the response and is immediately discarded.

3. Data Storage

Client information, payment records, and settings are stored in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

4. Third Party Services

  • Groq AI API — processes document requests (no data retained)
  • Cloudflare Workers — backend infrastructure
  • PayPal / GPay — payment processing (governed by their own policies)
  • Google Fonts — typography (standard CDN usage)

5. Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site. We do not maintain any personal data on our servers that requires deletion requests.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns: hellocadesk.ai@gmail.com

About CAdesk.ai

Version 2.0 · Built in India 🇮🇳

Our Mission

CAdesk.ai was built with one goal: to save Indian CA firms 3+ hours of drafting work every single day. We believe CAs should spend their time on strategy and client relationships, not repetitive document drafting.

What We Built

A complete AI-powered practice management dashboard that includes professional document generation, client management, deadline tracking, payment tracking, and WhatsApp message templates — all in one place, all free to start.

Why Indian CAs Specifically?

Generic AI tools don't understand Indian tax law, ICAI standards, GST regulations, or the specific language that Indian regulatory authorities expect. CAdesk.ai is trained on Indian legal language, real GST circulars, IT Act sections, and ICAI standards.
